About me

Dr. Mohamed Ahmed Nabawy

Assistant Professor
Ph.D. (GB), M.Sc., B.Sc.
D.BIM, D.CASL, D.AMP
M.APM (GB), M.ASCE (US), M.CMAA (US), M.IABSE (CH)

Dr. Mohamed earned his Ph.D. from London South Bank University (UK, London) in 2022. He earned his master degree from Arab Academy for Science and Technology in the field of construction engineering and management in 2014, and his Bachelor degree in 2008 in construction engineering and management. In addition. He have professional diplomas in Supply Chain Management and Logistics, Advanced Marketing Professional, and Building Information Modelling. He has a total Practical on Site and Academic Experience of 17 years in the field of construction engineering and management. He has practical experience background serving as a site civil engineer and technical site civil engineer in multinational companies. He worked as a contractor site engineer and technical engineer at companies of Arab Contactors since 2008 in Shoura Council project and Alexandria for Construction (TMG) since 2009 in Madinaty project as a Planner Engineer. He further worked as a Consultant Senior Civil Engineer at AAW Infrastructure Consultancy since 2010 to 2013 in Cairo Festival City project and Dora Group since 2013 in The Address Residential compound and Courtyard Retail projects. During his work as a senior consultant civil engineer, he managed the construction of sewage networks, water networks, irrigation networks, sewage pump station, water pump station, road works, and super structure activities. He is currently serving as an assistant professor at the British University in Egypt (BUE) since 2015. He taught various construction management modules. He published more than 30 papers in the field of construction management in international journals and in international conferences in London, France, UAE, and Egypt.
